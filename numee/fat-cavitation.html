What is Fat Cavitation?

Ultrasound fat cavitation is a non-surgical treatment that uses low-frequency sound waves to break down fat cells beneath the skin. The acoustic waves create microscopic bubbles in the fatty tissue, causing fat cells to rupture without damaging the surrounding skin or tissue.

Once broken down, the fat is naturally processed and eliminated by the body's lymphatic system over the following weeks. It is a body-sculpting method - best suited for targeting specific stubborn areas rather than as a general weight-loss solution.

How It Works

1

Free Consultation

We assess your goals and identify the right treatment areas and session count for your desired results.

2

30-60 Minute Session

A handheld device is moved across the treatment area with a conductive gel. You'll feel gentle warmth and mild vibration as the sound waves do their work.

3

Natural Fat Removal

Over the days following treatment, your body naturally processes and eliminates the disrupted fat cells through the lymphatic system.

4

Progressive Results (6-10 sessions)

Results are visible after 3-6 sessions, improving over time. A course of 6-10 weekly treatments is recommended for optimal results.
