What is HIFU?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical, non-invasive treatment that uses focused sound waves to heat targeted layers of tissue beneath the skin - stimulating the body's natural collagen production for lasting lift and tightening.

Unlike surface-level treatments, HIFU reaches the same tissue layers addressed in surgical facelifts - without any incisions, anaesthesia, or recovery time. The result is a naturally refreshed, lifted appearance that continues to improve over weeks.

7D HIFU vs 12D HIFU

We offer both 7D and 12D HIFU - each targeting a different number of tissue depths for varying levels of precision and results.

How It Works

1

Consultation

We assess your skin and goals to choose the right HIFU variant and treatment areas for you.

2

Treatment Session (45-60 mins)

A handpiece glides across the treatment area, delivering focused ultrasound energy. You may feel mild warmth - a sign of collagen activation.

3

Immediate Glow

Some tightening is visible straight away. Results improve progressively over 2-6 months as new collagen develops.

4

Lasting Results

Maximum tightening appears at around 6 months. Annual maintenance treatments help sustain your results.
