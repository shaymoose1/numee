HIFU facelift

Non-surgical skin lifting and tightening. Real results, zero downtime.

What Is HIFU?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical cosmetic treatment that uses ultrasound energy to lift and tighten the skin on the face, neck and décolletage. It targets the deep foundational layers of skin — the same layers addressed in a surgical facelift — stimulating natural collagen production without any incisions or injections.

At nu-mee clinic, we have over 8 years of experience delivering HIFU treatments with consistently outstanding results.

How Does It Work?

Focused ultrasound energy is delivered to precise depths beneath the skin's surface — typically 1.5mm, 3mm and 4.5mm — where it generates a controlled thermal effect. This heat triggers the body's natural healing response, stimulating the production of new collagen and elastin fibres. Over the following weeks and months, the skin gradually lifts, firms and tightens from within.

Each session typically takes 30–90 minutes depending on the area being treated. There is no anaesthetic required and you can return to normal activities immediately afterwards.

What Can HIFU Treat?

  • Sagging or loose skin on the face and neck
  • Jowls and loss of jawline definition
  • Drooping brows and hooded eyelids
  • Double chin and submental fullness
  • Fine lines and wrinkles around the eyes and forehead
  • Skin laxity on the décolletage

What Results Can I Expect?

Many clients notice an immediate tightening effect following their session. More significant lifting and firming develops progressively over 2–3 months as collagen remodelling continues. Results typically last 12–18 months, with many clients choosing a maintenance session once a year.

Results vary depending on skin condition, age and lifestyle — Lana will give you a realistic expectation during your free consultation.

Is There Any Downtime?

No downtime is required. Some clients experience mild redness or a slight tingling sensation for a few hours after the treatment, but this resolves quickly. You can apply makeup and resume all normal activities immediately.
