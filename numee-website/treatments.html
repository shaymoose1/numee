hifu non-surgical facelift

the gold standard in non-surgical skin lifting.

hifu (high intensity focused ultrasound) delivers controlled ultrasound energy deep beneath the skin's surface — targeting the same foundational layer typically addressed only by surgical facelifts, without any incisions. the focused heat stimulates your body's natural collagen production, resulting in lifted, firmer and more youthful-looking skin.

many clients notice an immediate tightening effect after their session, with results continuing to improve over the following 3–6 months as new collagen builds. most clients achieve optimal results with 1–2 sessions per year.

what happens during a session

a specialist handheld device is moved across the treatment area, delivering precise ultrasound pulses at multiple depth levels. a full face & neck session takes 45–60 minutes. you may feel a mild warmth or tingling — this is the ultrasound working at depth. there is no anaesthetic required and you can return to daily activities immediately.

ems body sculpting

thousands of muscle contractions — zero effort required.

ems (electrical muscle stimulation) body sculpting uses controlled low-frequency electrical impulses to trigger involuntary muscle contractions throughout the target area. these contractions are far more intense and comprehensive than those achievable through voluntary exercise, making ems uniquely effective for toning and defining muscle.

a single 40-minute session produces the equivalent of thousands of crunches or squats, stimulating deep muscle fibres that normal workouts often struggle to reach. it's completely painless — most clients describe a mild tingling or buzzing sensation — and requires no recovery time.

what happens during a session

electrode pads are applied directly to the skin over the target muscle group. the device sends precisely calibrated electrical pulses that cause the muscles to contract in rapid succession. sessions are 40 minutes per area. you'll leave feeling as though you've had a workout — without the sweat or exertion. there is no downtime and you can return to normal activities immediately.

melt your fat

fat cavitation

reshape stubborn areas without surgery or downtime.

fat cavitation uses low-frequency ultrasound waves to target and destroy subcutaneous fat cells — the stubborn layer of fat just beneath the skin's surface that is resistant to diet and exercise. the ultrasound creates microscopic bubbles (cavities) within the fat tissue; as these bubbles expand and collapse, they rupture the fat cell walls.

once the fat cell walls are disrupted, the cell contents (triglycerides) are released and gradually flushed out by the body's lymphatic system over the following days and weeks. the result is a gradual, natural reduction in circumference and an improvement in body contour.

what happens during a session

a conductive gel is applied to the area being treated. a handheld ultrasound device is moved across the skin in slow, circular movements for 30–60 minutes. the treatment is painless — most clients feel only a mild warmth. drinking plenty of water before and after each session helps the body eliminate released fat. a course of 6–10 weekly treatments is recommended for optimal results.
