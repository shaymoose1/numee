What Is Fat Cavitation?

Fat cavitation (also known as ultrasonic cavitation) is a non-surgical fat reduction treatment that uses low-frequency ultrasound waves to break down fat cells in targeted areas. The disrupted fat cells are then naturally processed and eliminated by the body's lymphatic system over the days following treatment.

At nu-mee clinic, Lana has been performing cavitation treatments for over 8 years, helping clients achieve significant, visible results without a single incision.

How Does It Work?

A handheld device is applied to the skin over the target area, delivering low-frequency ultrasound waves. These sound waves create rapid pressure changes that cause the fat cell membranes to rupture — a process called cavitation. The released fat (in the form of triglycerides) is then metabolised and eliminated by the body's lymphatic and urinary systems over the following days.

Sessions typically last 30–60 minutes depending on the area being treated. Multiple sessions are usually recommended for optimal results, and many clients combine cavitation with radio frequency (RF) skin tightening for enhanced body contouring.

Which Areas Can Be Treated?

  • Abdomen and love handles
  • Thighs (inner and outer)
  • Buttocks
  • Upper arms
  • Back and bra rolls
  • Chin and jawline (with suitable equipment)

What Results Can I Expect?

Most clients notice a visible reduction in the treated area after their first session, with results continuing to improve over the following week as the body eliminates the disrupted fat cells. Optimal results are typically seen after 6–10 sessions, and can be further enhanced by staying well hydrated and maintaining a healthy diet.

Cavitation is not a weight-loss treatment — it is most effective for targeting localised areas of stubborn fat that resist diet and exercise. Lana will assess your suitability and set realistic expectations during your free consultation.

Is There Any Downtime?

No downtime. Most clients experience no side effects at all. Mild redness or warmth in the treated area may occur immediately after the session, but this typically fades within an hour. You can return to all normal activities right away. Drinking plenty of water following each session helps to support the body's lymphatic elimination of fat cell debris.
