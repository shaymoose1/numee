understand your treatment

not sure where to start? here's everything you need to know about each of our treatments.

what is hifu?

hifu (high intensity focused ultrasound) is a non-surgical treatment that delivers precise ultrasound energy deep beneath the surface of the skin. this energy heats the tissue at a cellular level, triggering your body's natural healing response and stimulating new collagen production.

the result? lifted, tighter skin — without a single incision. many clients see an immediate visible improvement, with results continuing to build over the following 3–6 months as collagen regenerates.

how it works

a handheld device delivers focused ultrasound to multiple depth layers of the skin — including the deep foundational layer typically only reached by surgical facelifts. each session takes 45–60 minutes. there is no anaesthetic, no needles and no recovery time required.

what is ems body sculpting?

ems (electrical muscle stimulation) body sculpting uses controlled electrical impulses to trigger rapid, intense muscle contractions — far beyond what's possible through normal exercise. a single 40-minute session produces the equivalent of thousands of crunches or squats.

unlike traditional exercise, ems works the entire muscle simultaneously and at a depth that's difficult to reach manually. this leads to accelerated toning, definition and strengthening with zero physical exertion on your part.

how it works

electrode pads are applied to the target area. the device sends low-frequency electrical pulses that cause your muscles to contract involuntarily. sessions are 40 minutes, completely painless (a mild tingling sensation is normal), and require absolutely no downtime.

what is fat cavitation?

fat cavitation uses low-frequency ultrasound waves to target and break down stubborn subcutaneous fat cells beneath the skin. the ultrasound creates tiny bubbles within the fat tissue — these expand and implode, rupturing the fat cell walls.

the released fat content is then gradually eliminated naturally by the body's lymphatic system over the following days and weeks. this is a body contouring treatment — it reshapes and reduces targeted areas rather than acting as a weight-loss solution.

how it works

a gel is applied to the target area and a handheld ultrasound device is moved across the skin in circular motions. sessions take 30–60 minutes. a course of 6–10 weekly sessions is typically recommended for best results, as the body needs time to process eliminated fat between treatments.
