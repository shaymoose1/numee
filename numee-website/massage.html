About Our Ladies Massage

Our ladies massage is a professional, therapeutic relaxation massage tailored specifically for women. With 10 years of massage experience, Lana delivers a treatment that genuinely relieves tension, reduces stress and leaves you feeling completely restored.

Whether you're looking to unwind after a busy week, ease muscle tension, or simply take time for yourself — this is the treatment for you. The environment is calm, private and welcoming, designed exclusively for women.

What to Expect

Your massage session begins with a brief consultation to understand your areas of concern and any preferences you have — such as pressure level or focus areas. Lana will then tailor the massage to your specific needs during your session.

The treatment uses professional massage techniques including Swedish and deep tissue strokes to release tension across the full body, with particular attention to commonly tight areas such as the shoulders, neck, lower back and legs.

Benefits of Regular Massage

  • Relieves muscle tension and chronic tightness
  • Reduces stress, anxiety and mental fatigue
  • Improves circulation and lymphatic flow
  • Promotes better quality sleep
  • Supports recovery from physical activity
  • Provides a dedicated space for rest and self-care

Who Is It Suitable For?

Our ladies massage is suitable for most women. If you have any specific health conditions or concerns, please mention these when booking or during your consultation and Lana will adapt the treatment accordingly. Regular massage sessions are recommended for the best ongoing results — many clients visit fortnightly or monthly.
